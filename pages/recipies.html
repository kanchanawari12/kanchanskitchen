Chicken

  • Marinate the chicken with salt, turmeric, and red chili powder. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  • Heat butter in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  • Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  • Stir in cream and simmer for 5 minutes. Garnish with cilantro and serve with naan or rice.

Chole Bhature

  • Cook Chickpeas: Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt and water for 5-6 whistles (or until soft). If using canned chickpeas, rinse them well and skip this step.
  • Temper Spices: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • Add Base Flavors: Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for 1-2 minutes. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  • Cook Chickpeas with Masala: Add the boiled chickpeas and some of the cooking water (or plain water) to the masala. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  • Finish with Aromatics: Add chole masala, amchur, and kasuri methi. Mix well
